Welcome Player!

Minecraft-Pokemon Edition is a simplified version of the game Minecraft where you can catch pokemon, build, dig, mine, and more!

Use your axe to cut down trees and leaves.

Use your shovel to dig dirt and grass.

Use your pickaxe to mine rock.

Use your pokeball to catch pokemon.

There are six different types of pokemon for you to catch. The green health-bar on the right hand corner displays their health.

Attack the wild pokemon by selecting your pokemon and just click to weaken them. When their health is low, use your Pokeball to capture them
